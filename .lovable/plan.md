
## Math Lesson: "The Smoothie Shop" — Ratios & Percentages

A story-driven interactive explorer where the student runs a smoothie shop and learns ratios & percentages through hands-on activities.

### Story Setup
- Welcome screen introducing the student as the new owner of "Berry Bliss Smoothie Shop"
- Warm, colorful design with purples, pinks, and soft gradients — friendly and non-intimidating
- A guide character ("Maya") who narrates and encourages throughout

### Three Interactive Scenes

**Scene 1 — "Mix the Perfect Smoothie" (Ratios)**
- Draggable sliders to mix fruit ingredients (e.g. 2 parts strawberry : 1 part banana)
- Visual glass fills up showing the ratio in real-time
- Maya explains what ratios mean using the mix the student just created
- Mini-challenge: "A customer wants the same taste but a BIGGER cup — adjust the amounts!"

**Scene 2 — "Today's Sales" (Percentages)**
- Interactive pie chart showing today's smoothie sales by flavor
- Student clicks slices to see how many out of total = what percent
- Animated number transitions as they explore
- Mini-challenge: "What percentage of sales were mango?"

**Scene 3 — "Discount Day!" (Applying Percentages)**
- Price tags on smoothies; student uses a slider to set a discount (10%, 25%, 50%)
- Prices update live showing original → discounted price
- Maya walks through the mental math: "50% of $6 is just half!"
- Mini-challenge: "Set the right discount so the $8 smoothie costs $6"

### UX Details
- Progress bar at top showing Scene 1 → 2 → 3
- Encouraging feedback on every interaction ("Nice job!", "You're getting it!", celebratory confetti on completion)
- "Hint" button available on every challenge (no penalty)
- Final congratulations screen with a summary of what they learned
- Fully responsive for tablet/desktop use
